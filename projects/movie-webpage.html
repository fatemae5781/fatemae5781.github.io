movie webpage Project

Context

For my SEP 10 project, I worked on creating a movie webpage about my story titled "Welcome to the Haunted Island." The goal was to apply what I learned about basic HTML and CSS to develop a webpage that tells a spooky story through text and images. The project took about two weeks, including planning, and coding. I worked with a partner to combine our ideas and skills, making sure we met all the requirements such as using headings, paragraphs, divs, spans, input fields, Google Fonts, and CSS selectors. We also had to include images, create a plan using markdown, and organize who did what in the project.

Content

I made a webpage that tells the story of a person trapped on a haunted island, describing their experiences each day leading up to their escape. The webpage includes several sections with days and events, a spooky background, and styled text to enhance the creepy atmosphere. I added images of the island,and someone escaping,One of my main goals was to make a specific word in a sentence stand out with a red and white color using spans. I also tried to add a new Google Font, but I found it tricky at first and had to look up tutorials on how to include multiple fonts in my project.

Reflection:

Challenges

I faced several challenges during this project. One difficulty was figuring out how to center my images properly, especially when I had other styles like padding or margin that interfered. I also struggled with using spans to make one word in a sentence red and white, which took some experimenting with CSS and HTML. Also, I found it hard to add a new Google Font because I wasn’t sure how to include it in the html page and there was an error. Another challenge was merging my vision with my partner’s, since we had different ideas about how the story should look and flow. It took time to communicate and combine our ideas into one good story.

Takeaways

From this experience, I learned that patience and persistence are important when coding, especially when trying new techniques. I realized that asking for help and searching for tutorials online or google is very useful and looking through your notes and freecodecamp would be very helpful. I also learned the importance of good communication when working with a partner to share ideas and make adjustments. Planning is crucial, especially when combining different visions, to make sure everything fits well.

Next Steps

In the future, I want to improve my skills in styling images and adding more creative elements to my webpage. I also plan to practice adding multiple Google Fonts more easily and explore different ways to make my webpage more visually appealing. Additionally, I want to learn how to organize my code better. I am proud of what I accomplished and look forward to making more complex and engaging website
